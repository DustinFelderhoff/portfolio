Weekly AI News Recap

July 19, 2026 — The week's top stories in AI, product management, and tech.

Moonshot AI's Kimi K3: Open-Weight Giant Dethrones Claude

Moonshot AI released Kimi K3, a 2.8-trillion parameter mixture-of-experts model — the largest open-weight release in history. It surged to #1 on Frontend Code Arena, beating Claude Fable 5 with a 76% pairwise win rate. Scores 88.3 on Terminal Bench 2.1, just behind GPT-5.6 Sol. Architecture: 896 total experts (16 active/request), 1M-token context window with Kimi Delta Attention (6.3x faster decoding). Pricing: $3/M input, $15/M output tokens — 2–3x cheaper than Western flagships for coding workloads. Open weights expected July 27. Elevated hallucination rate noted. Hong Kong IPO planned.
