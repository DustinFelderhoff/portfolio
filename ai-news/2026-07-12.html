🚀 OpenAI

**GPT-5.6 ships in three tiers: Sol, Terra, Luna** OpenAI released GPT-5.6 with flagship Sol (state-of-the-art 80 on Coding Agent Index), intermediate Terra, and budget Luna. Sol's "ultra mode" coordinates four parallel agents via Programmatic Tool Calling, cutting cyber benchmark token usage by a third. Sol outperforms Anthropic's Fable 5 at one-third the cost and is now the preferred model for Microsoft 365 Copilot. API pricing: Luna $1, Sol $5 per million input tokens.
**ChatGPT Work launches across mobile and desktop** Automates multi-step documentation workflows. OpenAI's standalone Atlas browser retires August 9 in favor of this integrated approach. OpenAI also acquired applied-AI consulting startup Northslope to accelerate enterprise deployment.
**GPT-Live voice overhaul** Full-duplex architecture allowing natural interruptions — voice engine keeps conversation flowing while GPT-5.5 handles deep reasoning and web searches in the background.
**Internal shakeup: Fidji Simo → advisory role** AGI lead Fidji Simo is stepping back due to a chronic illness. President Greg Brockman takes charge of the newly unified agent and product strategy.
