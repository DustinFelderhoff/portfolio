2. US Government Eases Restrictions on Anthropic's Fable 5

Sources: AI Breakfast, The Rundown AI The Department of Commerce officially lifted export controls on Anthropic's top-tier systems, paving the way for Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 to return after a multi-week ban over jailbreak concerns. Anthropic may introduce strict KYC identity verification for Fable 5 access. GPT-5.6 could follow soon after.

3. Claude Sonnet 5 Launches — 1M Token Context, But Costs Add Up

Source: AI Breakfast Anthropic dropped Claude Sonnet 5 as its new default mid-tier model with a 1M token context window, real-time cyber safeguards, and five reasoning effort settings. However, independent benchmarking shows it can cost more per completed task than Opus 4.8 due to the sheer volume of generated text. Promotional pricing at $2/M input, $10/M output through August.
